More about Dr Rupali Kar

An orthopedic specialist is a medical doctor with extensive training in diagnosing, treating, and preventing disorders of the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. They manage a variety of conditions, from fractures and dislocations to chronic diseases like arthritis and osteoporosis. Orthopedic specialists perform surgeries such as joint replacements, arthroscopic procedures, and fracture repairs, utilizing both traditional and minimally invasive techniques. In addition to surgical interventions, they develop comprehensive treatment plans that may include physical therapy, medication, and lifestyle modifications to promote recovery and improve quality of life. These specialists also play a crucial role in sports medicine, helping athletes recover from injuries and enhance their performance. They work closely with patients of all ages, from children with congenital conditions to elderly individuals with degenerative diseases. Orthopedic specialists often collaborate with other healthcare professionals to provide holistic care and are dedicated to advancing their field through research and innovation. Their expertise and commitment are essential for restoring mobility and alleviating pain, significantly impacting patients' lives.
